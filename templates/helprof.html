Here is the explanation of the pages:

Exams Creation:
On this page, the professor can generate the exams of their choice and the respective components.
Tests Dates Assignment:
On this page, the professor can determine the dates on which the previously created exams will take place.
Tests Created:
On this page, the professor can view and modify the list of exams they have created, along with the assigned dates.
Tests Grade Registration:
On this page, the professor will find a list of students who participated in their exam, allowing them to input the assigned grades.
Students Status Records:
On this page, the professor can access a list of students along with their completed exams, even if the grades are insufficient, as long as the validity period is still active.
Tests Grade Registration:
On this page, the professor can view the list of students eligible for exam grade registration and proceed to record the grades.
Account:
On this page, the professor can log in to access their profile.
Help:
This page provides explanations for all menu pages and outlines specific rules that the professor must adhere to.
